\App{} and \OtherApp{} are parts of a chain of tools to assemble images.

\begin{itemize}
\item
  \application{Enblend} combines a series of pictures taken at the same location but in
  different directions.

\item
  \application{Enfuse} merges photos of the same subject at the same location and same
  direction, but taken with varying exposure parameters.
\end{itemize}
